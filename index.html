Honeywell Law

Barrister and Solicitor

William Honeywell of Honeywell Law provides experienced legal representation. He has a broad legal background, which includes experience in many areas of real estate law, including extensive experience in condominium law.

Mr. Honeywell has worked extensively in the wills and estates area. He has drafted both simple and sophisticated wills for his many clients. He has also custom-designed many complex estate and succession plans for clients. A complete estate plan includes as a minimum a will, powers of attorney for both property and personal care, and a living will. Succession planning may include the use of either inter vivos or testamentary trusts.

Mr. Honeywell has led seminars and lectured on a variety of legal subjects for various organizations, including the Canadian Bar Association, the County of Carleton Law Association, the Law Society of Upper Canada and the University of Ottawa. Mr. Honeywell received his B.A. from the University of Guelph in 1969 and both his M.B.A. and LL.B. from Dalhousie University in 1975. He was called to the Ontario bar in 1977.

Gillian Thomas of Honeywell Law is a law clerk with 30 years of paralegal experience and is known for her exceptional client service.

Jess Honeywell has recently joined Honeywell Law as a paralegal.

Welcome to GitHub Pages.

This automatic page generator is the easiest way to create beautiful pages for all of your projects. Author your page content here using GitHub Flavored Markdown, select a template crafted by a designer, and publish. After your page is generated, you can check out the new branch:

$ cd your_repo_root/repo_name
$ git fetch origin
$ git checkout gh-pages

If you're using the GitHub for Mac, simply sync your repository and you'll see the new branch.

Designer Templates

We've crafted some handsome templates for you to use. Go ahead and continue to layouts to browse through them. You can easily go back to edit your page before publishing. After publishing your page, you can revisit the page generator and switch to another theme. Your Page content will be preserved if it remained markdown format.

Rather Drive Stick?

If you prefer to not use the automatic generator, push a branch named gh-pages to your repository to create a page manually. In addition to supporting regular HTML content, GitHub Pages support Jekyll, a simple, blog aware static site generator written by our own Tom Preston-Werner. Jekyll makes it easy to create site-wide headers and footers without having to copy them across every page. It also offers intelligent blog support and other advanced templating features.
